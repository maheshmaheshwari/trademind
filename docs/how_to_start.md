### Step 1 — Register Your Company (Day 1 Blocker)

You cannot raise on any platform without a legal entity.  
YC, 100x.vc, and Tyke all require a registered company to process your application.

**Fastest option:** Private Limited Company registration

| Detail | Info |
|---|---|
| Platform | Razorpay Rize (rize.razorpay.com) or Indiafilings.com |
| Cost | ₹6,000–₹10,000 (all-inclusive) |
| Time | 7–10 days, fully online |
| Documents needed | PAN card, Aadhaar, one address proof |
| Company name suggestion | TradeMind Technologies Pvt Ltd |

> **If you already have a company registered — skip this step.**

**Why Private Limited (not LLP or OPC):**
- VCs and angels only invest in Pvt Ltd companies
- Equity issuance (ESOPs, investor shares) only works in Pvt Ltd
- LLP and OPC cannot raise institutional funding
