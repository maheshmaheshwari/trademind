🏗️ Architecture

Data Layer: Automated collectors fetch prices, news, and indicators daily at 4 PM IST.

AI Layer: 6 trained ML models score every Nifty 500 stock. Signals are stored with confidence scores.

Execution Layer: Paper trading engine with bracket orders, P&L tracking, and risk management.

Database: ENV-based routing — local SQLite for development, Turso cloud sync for production.
